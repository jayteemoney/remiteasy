## ğŸ¨ Quick Start: Create Logo in Canva

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Go to Canva.com**
   - Create free account
   - Search "Logo" templates

2. **Choose Template:**
   - Look for tech/fintech logos
   - Minimal, modern style
   - Square or horizontal layout

3. **Customize:**
   - Replace text with "CasperFlow"
   - Use Inter or Poppins font (bold)
   - Apply color scheme: #FF0011 (red), #0066FF (blue)

4. **Add Ghost Icon:**
   - Search Canva elements: "ghost friendly"
   - Or use basic shapes to create:
     - Circle (head)
     - Rounded rectangle (body)
     - Two small circles (eyes)
     - Small arc (smile)
     - Wavy lines (flow)

5. **Apply Gradient:**
   - Select flow lines
   - Add gradient: #0066FF â†’ #00D4FF

6. **Export:**
   - Download PNG (transparent, 1024px)
   - Download SVG (if Canva Pro)
   - Download PDF (print version)
