## Presentation Tips

### Delivery (5 minutes)

**Timing Breakdown:**
- Slides 1-3 (Problem): 1 min
- Slides 4-6 (Solution): 1.5 min
- Slides 7-8 (Market/Tech): 1 min
- Slides 9-10 (Demo/Impact): 1 min
- Slides 11-12 (Future/CTA): 0.5 min

### Speaking Notes

**Opening:**
> "Imagine sending money home to family and losing 10% to fees. For someone sending $200, that's $20 gone - enough to buy a week's groceries. This affects 1.7 billion people worldwide."

**Demo:**
> "Let me show you how RemitEasy solves this. [Live demo or video]. Notice how simple it is - just a phone number, no complex crypto terms."

**Impact:**
> "This isn't just about saving money - it's about financial inclusion. We're enabling families to support each other during medical emergencies, pay school fees, and build better futures."

**Closing:**
> "RemitEasy is ready to deploy on Celo mainnet. We invite you to try it, contribute to the open-source code, and join us in making remittances accessible to everyone."

### Q&A Prep

**Expected Questions:**

1. **Q: How do you make money?**
   A: 0.5% platform fee on successful remittances. Sustainable at scale.

2. **Q: What about regulatory compliance?**
   A: Phase 2 includes KYC integration. Initially targeting regions with crypto-friendly regulations.

3. **Q: How is this different from existing crypto remittance services?**
   A: MiniPay phone resolution, group contributions, and ultra-low fees (0.5% vs 2-5%).

4. **Q: What if the target isn't met?**
   A: Creator can cancel and all contributors automatically get refunds via smart contract.

5. **Q: Security concerns?**
   A: OpenZeppelin audited libraries, 100% test coverage, reentrancy protection, verified on Celoscan.
